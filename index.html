About Me

Hello World! I'm Grant, a speedcuber from SoCal, student at UC Berkeley, and the creator of this website! I still remember picking up my first cube in 3rd grade and falling in love with this intricate puzzle. There is some magic when I place all six colors in their correct spots: the crisp feeling turning each piece swiftly. Since learning how to solve a 3x3, I've challenged myself to solve many other puzzles as highlighted on this website and also become a speedcube competitor. My favorite competition experience was at the Rubik's Cube World Championships in Brazil in 2015! I got the opportunity to meet a ton of amazing people and really immerse myself in the incredible cubing community. My goal is to continue spreading my passion for cubing to others. Solving a Rubik's Cube really doesn't take a high IQ, it just takes patience and a willingness/passion to learn. I hope you can discover interest through my website, and thank you for visiting!
